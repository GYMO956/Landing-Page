GYMO App - Privacy Policy (India)

This Privacy Policy outlines how GYMO ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, shares, and protects the personal information of users and gyms through the GYMO mobile application ("App") and associated services in accordance with Indian regulations.

  1. Information Collection

    • User Information:

      We collect personal information provided by users during the registration process, including but not limited to name, contact details, and payment information.

    • Gym Information:

      We collect personal information provided by users during the registration process, including but not limited to name, contact details, and payment information.

  2. Usage of Information

    • User Information:

      User information is used to facilitate the booking of gym sessions, process payments, and communicate important updates related to the App and services.

    • Gym Information:

      Gym information is used to verify the legitimacy of gyms on the platform and ensure compliance with applicable regulations.

  3. Data Security

    • We collect personal information provided by users during the registration process, including but not limited to name, contact details, and payment information.

    • Users and gyms are encouraged to choose strong and unique passwords for their accounts.

  4. Information Sharing

    • With Gyms:

      User information necessary for the booking process, such as name and contact details, may be shared with gyms.

    • With Third Parties:

      We may share aggregated and anonymized information with third parties for analytics and improvement of our services.

    • Legal Requirements

      We may disclose personal information if required by law or in response to a valid legal process.

  5. User Control

    • Users can review and update their personal information through the App.

    • Users can opt-out of promotional communications by adjusting their notification preferences.

  6. Cookies and Tracking

    • We may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance user experience and gather information about usage patterns.

  7. Changes to the Privacy Policy

    • We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Users will be notified of changes, and continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of the modified policy.

  8. Contact Information

    • For any inquiries or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, users and gyms can contact us at [admin@gymo.co.in/+91 8099778567].

  9. Governing Law

    • This Privacy Policy is governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of India.

By using the GYMO App, users and gyms acknowledge that they have read, understood, and agree to this Privacy Policy.
